freund

freund is a staged work developed at MUK.theater in Vienna. It explores intimacy, distance, and the instability of shared presence.

Through repetition and minimal variation, gestures begin to shift in meaning, revealing subtle tensions between proximity and separation.

The work unfolds as a slow accumulation of actions, where duration itself becomes a structural element.
